Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our premium photography services.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking 6-12 months in advance for weddings, especially for popular dates. Portrait sessions can typically be booked 2-4 weeks in advance. Contact us to check our availability for your specific date.

What's included in the wedding packages?

All wedding packages include professional photography coverage, edited high-resolution digital images, online gallery with download access, and a consultation session. Higher packages include additional photographers, albums, prints, and videography services.

Do you travel for shoots?

Yes! We frequently travel throughout Tamil Nadu and across India for weddings and commercial assignments. Travel expenses are calculated based on location and included in your custom quote. Destination weddings are our specialty!

How long does editing take?

Wedding galleries are typically delivered within 6-8 weeks. Portrait sessions are delivered in 2-3 weeks. We believe in quality over speed - each image receives individual attention to ensure it meets our artistic standards.

Can we customize a package?

Absolutely! We understand every client has unique needs. Contact us to discuss custom packages tailored specifically to your requirements, budget, and vision. We offer à la carte services as well.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, UPI payments, credit/debit cards, and cash. A 30% deposit is required to secure your date, with the balance due one week before the event. We also offer payment plans for larger packages.

Do you provide raw/unedited photos?

We deliver professionally edited high-resolution images. We do not provide raw files as our editing is an integral part of our artistic process and final product. All delivered images are fully retouched and color-corrected.

What happens in case of bad weather?

We always have backup plans! We can reschedule outdoor shoots or move to indoor locations. For weddings, we're prepared with professional lighting and equipment to handle any weather conditions while maintaining quality.
